# Doshas — what they are, and when to actually worry

People ask about these by name. Be honest: most doshas are **common, often cancelled, and remediable** — don't alarm, don't monetise fear. For a serious case (marriage, persistent trouble), send them to a human pandit with the full chart.

| Dosha | What it is | Matters for | Reality check |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Manglik / Mangal dosha** | Mars in 1/2/4/7/8/12 from lagna (also Moon/Venus) | Marriage mainly; minor for business | ~50% have some form. Many **cancellations** (Mars in own/exalted sign; both partners Manglik; eases after ~28). Not a business blocker |
| **Sade Sati** | Saturn over 12th/1st/2nd from Moon (~7.5 yrs) | A demanding phase | Tests, doesn't destroy. Work hard, stay humble, Saturn remedies. See `dasha-transits.md` |
| **Kaal Sarp** | All planets between Rahu & Ketu | General obstacles | Debated even among astrologers; partial KSD is mild. Remedy: Shiva/Nag puja |
| **Pitra dosha** | Afflicted 9th / Sun–Rahu | Ancestral karma, family-line delays | Remedy: Shradh, Pitru-Paksha tarpan, serve elders |
| **Nadi / Bhakoot** | In match-making | Partnerships/marriage | The real dealbreakers in matching (`compatibility.md`) |

**Reading a person's doshas:** `scripts/kundli.py` flags **Manglik** and **Sade Sati** from birth data. Check it, give the honest severity (usually "common, manageable, here's the cheap remedy"), and only escalate to a human pandit for serious or marriage cases. A business is rarely blocked by a personal dosha — say so.

**Rule for answering:** name the dosha plainly → give the *honest* severity (usually "common, manageable") → the cheap remedy → and when a human pandit is genuinely worth it. Never use fear.
