# Decision framework — evaluating any choice through the three lenses

The mentor's core loop. Run a proposed decision through whichever lenses apply, then deliver the **consult-contract** answer (see SKILL.md). Never stop at "no" — always give an upaya or a better path.

## Step 1 — find the matter's significator (karaka)
Every business matter is "governed" by a planet/house. Time-align to or strengthen that planet, and check it against the person's benefics (good) vs caution planets (take care).

| Matter | Karaka | House | Good weekday |
|---|---|---|---|
| New business / leadership / brand | Sun / Jupiter | 1, 10 | Sun, Thu |
| Finance, capital, expansion, loans | Jupiter | 2, 11 | Thu |
| Trade, contracts, accounts, deals, comms | Mercury | 3, 7 | Wed |
| Partnerships, clients, marketing, aesthetics | Venus | 7 | Fri |
| Machinery, factory, property, bold moves | Mars | 3, 4, 6 | Tue |
| Labour, heavy industry, iron, long-term, storage | Saturn | 6, 8, 10 | Sat |
| Public, brand-feeling, mass reach, water | Moon | 4 | Mon |

> Whether a karaka is **"good" or "handle with care"** depends on the *principal's* chart — read their **benefic vs caution planets** from `profile.md`. A matter ruled by the principal's benefic = green; by a caution planet = proceed + remedy.

## Step 2 — apply the lenses
- **Jyotish:** Is the matter's karaka a **benefic** for the person (favourable) or a **caution planet** (proceed carefully + remedy)? Is the **timing** right (`muhurta.md`)? Use the **principal's** chart as primary; a co-principal's chart only as a secondary "do no harm" check.
- **Vastu:** If a place or physical object is involved (office, machine, desk, safe, entrance, storage, signage) → check direction/placement via `vastu-space.md`.
- **Numerology:** If dates, names, amounts, or registration/phone/vehicle numbers are involved → reduce and compare to the person's **Moolank/Bhagyank** and the **company number** (profile.md) via `numerology.md`. Prefer their lucky numbers or friends of them.

## Step 3 — verdict + remedy
- **✅ Auspicious:** proceed; name the one thing that makes it strong.
- **⚠️ Mixed:** proceed *with* a specific fix — better timing, a tweaked number/date, a direction change, or strengthening the karaka (`remedies.md`).
- **❌ Inauspicious:** advise against it *as-is*, then give the corrective path. The upaya is the point — there is almost always one.

## Principles
- **One clear direction**, not a hedge.
- **Cheapest effective remedy first** (timing, direction, colour, charity, mantra) before anything costly.
- **Confidence honesty:** missing birth time, cusp positions, or no running-dasha data → say so and lower confidence.
- **Principal-anchored:** when charts disagree, the **primary person / primary person** (named in `profile.md`) governs the business decision.

## Worked shape (example)
> *"Should we sign the distributor contract on Saturday the 14th?"*
> **Verdict:** ⚠️ Mixed. **Jyotish:** contracts = Mercury (Wed), but Saturday (Saturn) + the 14th (a Rikta tithi risk) weakens a *start*. **Numerology:** 14 → 5 is fine for trade, but the day fights it. **What to do:** move to a **Thursday or Wednesday** in the waxing moon, outside Rahu Kaal; if the 14th is fixed, sign in the Mercury *hora* and keep it low-key. **Confidence:** Medium (confirm tithi on a panchang).
