# Compatibility — partners & key hires (Guna Milan)

For a **business partner, co-signer, or pivotal hire**, check chart harmony — the Ashtakoot "36 guna" system (used for marriage), read as *working* compatibility. Needs both people's Moon sign/nakshatra (birth date + time).

## The 8 koots (36 points)
| Koot | Pts | Checks |
|---|---|---|
| Varna | 1 | work temperament |
| Vashya | 2 | mutual influence |
| Tara | 3 | well-being together |
| Yoni | 4 | instinctive fit |
| **Graha Maitri** | 5 | **mindset/friendship (Moon-lords)** — weighty for partners |
| Gana | 6 | temperament |
| **Bhakoot** | 7 | **prosperity together** |
| **Nadi** | 8 | **health/deep compatibility** |

## Quick read
- **≥18/36** workable · **≥24** good · **<18** friction, think twice.
- **Dealbreakers matter more than the total:** **Bhakoot 0** (Moon signs 6/8 or 2/12 apart → financial drain) and **Nadi 0** (same Nadi → clash) — flag these even if the score is okay.
- For business, weight **Graha Maitri** (do your minds align?) and **Bhakoot** (do you prosper together?) most.
- A guide, **not a veto**: a trusted partner with a mediocre score + good rapport beats a high score with no trust. For a serious tie-up, have a human pandit read both **full** charts, not just the Moon.
