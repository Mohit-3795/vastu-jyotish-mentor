## Moon sign & nakshatra (the key driver)

The Moon moves ≈ 13°20′/day, so it changes **rashi every ≈ 2.25 days** and **nakshatra every ≈ 1 day**. With an approximate birth time you can usually fix the Moon *sign*; the *nakshatra* may be borderline. If the birth date is near a sign change, compute **both** candidate signs, flag it, and recommend a panchang/ephemeris check. Without an ephemeris, estimate and state the uncertainty, do not present a guess as exact.

### Colour-selection logic

1. **Anchor** the palette on the power planets' colours (especially any yogakaraka and the lagna lord).
2. **Balance** across the Pancha Bhuta, don't let one element dominate (see `vastu-logo.md`).
3. **Strengthen** a weak/needed planet by featuring its colour; **moderate** a caution planet to a small accent or drop it.
4. **Moon sign ruler + Sun** colours are always safe supporting tones.
5. Resolve clashes toward the person's stated aesthetic: satisfy the rule with a refined tint/shade rather than a loud primary.
