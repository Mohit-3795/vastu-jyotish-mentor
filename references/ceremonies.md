# Ceremonies & pujas for a Hindu business

Match the act to its rite. Keep advice practical, a local priest does the detailed vidhi; you give the *what, when, and why*.

| Occasion | Puja | Deity | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| Breaking ground on a site/factory | **Bhumi Pujan / Shilanyas** | Bhumi Devi, Ganesha, Vastu Purusha, Naga | On a muhurta; start at the **NE**; before any construction |
| Moving into office/factory | **Griha / Vastu Pravesh** | Ganesha, Lakshmi, Vastu Purusha | Boil milk till it overflows (= abundance); enter **right foot first** on a muhurta |
| **Tools, machines, equipment** | **Vishwakarma Puja / Ayudha Puja** | **Vishwakarma** (the divine engineer) | **Core for you as a manufacturer.** Vishwakarma Puja ≈ **17 Sep** yearly; Ayudha Puja at Dussehra. Clean & worship the machines; don't run them that day |
| Starting / daily blessing | **Lakshmi-Ganesh Puja** | Ganesha + Lakshmi + Kubera | **Ganesha first, always.** Kubera/north for the cash box |
| New vehicle / delivery truck | **Vahan Puja** | Ganesha | Nimbu-mirchi, lemon under the wheel, on a good day |
| New account books / fin-year | **Chopda / Bahi Pujan** | Lakshmi, Saraswati | At Diwali |
| Inauguration / opening | Ganesh puja + ribbon on muhurta | Ganesha, Lakshmi | Avoid Rahu Kaal; a simple sankalp is enough if no priest |

**Deities for business, at a glance:** **Ganesha** = beginnings & removing obstacles (first, always) · **Lakshmi** = wealth/cash-flow · **Kubera** = treasury/savings (north) · **Vishwakarma** = tools/production/quality · **Saraswati** = skill/design/knowledge · **Hanuman** = protection & Saturn relief.

**Tone:** suggest the rite + a good day + the spirit of it. Keep it light and doable; never imply doom if a step is skipped.
