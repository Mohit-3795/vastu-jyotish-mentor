# Vastu for the business, office, factory, desk, directions

Map the premises to 8 directions (top = North). Each governs a domain; place each function in its friendly zone, keep the **centre (Brahmasthan) open**, and keep the **NE (Ishan) light, clean, and uncluttered**.

| Direction | Governs | Put here | Keep out |
|---|---|---|---|
| **N** (Mercury / Kubera) | Wealth flow, opportunity, cash | Accounts, cash, treasury; face N while working | Heavy clutter, toilets |
| **NE / Ishan** (Water) | Clarity, fortune, "the head" | Water, light, clean open space, a think/prayer corner | Toilets, heavy storage, machinery, clutter |
| **E** (Sun) | Growth, authority, entry | Main entrance, windows, light | Blockage, tall heavy mass |
| **SE / Agni** (Fire / Venus) | Energy, finance heat | Furnaces, electrical, boilers, heat machinery, pantry | Water tanks, underground water |
| **S** (Mars) | Strength, reputation | Heavy machinery, tall storage | Main entrance (less ideal) |
| **SW / Nairutya** (Earth) | Stability, control | **Principal/MD seat (facing NE/N/E), solid wall behind**; heaviest mass; safe/vault | Openings, toilets, voids, lightness |
| **W** (Saturn) | Gains, output, storage | Finished-goods staging, storage, dining |, |
| **NW / Vayu** (Air) | Movement, clients, sales | Sales/marketing, logistics, dispatch, reception, finished goods (so stock moves out) | Principal's seat (too "moving") |

## Factory specifics
- **Principal / MD:** SW block; seat **facing NE / N / E**; solid wall behind; entrance not at their back.
- **Heavy machinery / furnace / heat:** SE, or S/SW. **Raw material:** SW / W. **Finished goods:** NW (so stock flows out).
- **Entrance:** prefer N, E, NE. **Accounts / cash:** N or NE-facing; drawer opening toward N/E.
- **Toilets:** NW, W, or S, **never NE, never the centre.**
- **Centre (Brahmasthan):** keep open and light, no heavy machine, pillar, or storage dumped in the middle.

## Desk / seat quick rules
- Face **North or East** while working; **solid support behind** you (no window at your back).
- Don't sit under an exposed beam; don't face a sharp wall corner; the door should not be directly behind you.
- Keep the desk's NE corner clear (water / crystal / small plant ok); heavy items to the SW.

## Quick fixes (more in `remedies.md`)
- Wrong-direction entrance → add light, a clean threshold marker, a symbolic boundary.
- Clutter or toilet in NE → keep spotless and closed, a bowl of rock salt, regular sea-salt cleaning.
- Principal can't sit SW → at minimum, a solid wall behind and face N/E.

## Plot & building selection (for a new site/factory)
- **Shape:** square or rectangle (length ≤ ~2× width). Avoid triangular, L-shaped, many-cornered plots. **NE-extended** = auspicious; **SW/SE-cut** = not.
- **Slope/level:** ground sloping **down toward N/E/NE** (higher in S/W/SW) = wealth & health. Avoid high-NE / low-SW.
- **Roads:** roads on N and/or E favourable. A **road-hit (Veedhi Shoola)** on the **SW** is the worst; on N/E it's mild.
- **Water:** borewell/underground tank in **NE**; overhead tank **SW/W**. Effluent/septic SW or NW, never NE.
- **Entrance/gate:** N, E, or NE. *Get the plot right first, far cheaper than remedying a bad one later.*

## When you can't change it (Vastu-dosha fixes)
- Stuck orientation → **face N/E, solid support behind**, keep NE light.
- Toilet/clutter in NE → spotless, closed, rock-salt bowl, sea-salt mopping.
- Missing/cut corner → mirror, brass/wood pyramid, or a heavy plant to "complete" it.
- Heavy NE / void SW → shift weight to SW, lighten NE.
- Persistent trouble → a **Vastu Shanti puja** beats breaking walls.
